## 3 — The Object and the Silence

The object—3I/ATLAS—moved through the solar wind like a rumor through a family. Every instrument had an accent; every observer, a bias. Some saw a line. Some saw a teardrop. One saw a ship. It was my job not to fall in love with any version. Love is allowed. It is not a method.

Silence is its own instrument. When the big telescopes went quiet, we logged the quiet as data. When the amateurs spoke, we logged their voices with the same calm pen: source, date, processing, evidence tier. Nothing sacred; nothing dismissed. "Trust the partner; verify the data." It sounds like a compromise. It is a discipline.
